About VaultRail

A simpler bridge between cards and crypto.

VaultRail is a concept product: one clean interface for moving from a debit or credit card into a handful of major cryptocurrencies, without the usual maze of exchanges and wallets.

Why we're building this

Most people who want to buy crypto for the first time give up somewhere between "create an exchange account" and "wire a bank transfer." VaultRail's concept collapses that into three screens: add a card, pick a coin, confirm an amount.

This build is a front-end prototype used to walk stakeholders through that experience before any backend, compliance, or banking integration work begins.

What this prototype demonstrates

  • 01

    Card recognition

    Live detection of Visa, Mastercard, Amex and Verve from the card number as it's typed.

  • 02

    A familiar dashboard

    Balance check, crypto purchase and bank transfer, presented as equal, parallel actions.

  • 03

    A real purchase flow

    Coin selection, an amount-to-quantity calculator, an order summary, and a confirmation step.

  • 04

    Production-style polish

    The visual language — typography, motion, micro-copy — is built to the bar of a shipped product.
